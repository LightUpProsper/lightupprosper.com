About us

Local students. Premium lighting. Honest work.

Light Up Prosper was created to provide homeowners with beautiful landscape lighting while helping local students learn business, craftsmanship, and customer service.

A local business with a bigger purpose.

Our team is made up of hardworking Prosper-area student athletes who take pride in doing the job right. Every project is planned carefully and completed under experienced adult supervision.

We believe in clear communication, fair pricing, premium products, and treating every home as if it were our own.

When you choose Light Up Prosper, you are investing in your home and helping local students gain valuable real-world experience.
