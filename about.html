About Us

Meet the Team

Light Up Prosper is owned and operated by local students who are learning what it means to build a business through hard work, craftsmanship, and service.

Built by Students. Backed by Family.

Light Up Prosper was started by a group of local students who wanted to build something of their own while serving homeowners in Prosper and nearby communities.

Supported by their families, mentors, and supplier partners, they are gaining real-world experience while creating beautiful outdoor spaces.

The adults help with guidance, safety, transportation, and business support. The students carry the vision, the effort, and the pride in the work.
