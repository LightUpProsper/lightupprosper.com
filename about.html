About us

Built for homes. Built for students.

Light Up Prosper provides beautiful landscape lighting while giving local student athletes real-world experience in service, responsibility, and craftsmanship.

Our story

Light Up Prosper was founded to provide homeowners with clean, premium landscape lighting while helping local students learn practical skills that last beyond the job.

Our team is made up of hardworking Prosper-area student athletes. Every installation is completed with adult supervision, attention to detail, and respect for the homeowner’s property.

What we believe

Show up on time
Do clean, honest work
Use quality lighting products
Treat every yard like our own
Build confidence in local students
