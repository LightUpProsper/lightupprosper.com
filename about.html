About us

Meet the Founders

Light Up Prosper was founded by three local student entrepreneurs with a simple goal: build a business they could be proud of while serving their community. Every lighting plan is carefully designed before installation. Light Up Prosper was created to provide beautiful, professionally installed landscape lighting while giving local student athletes a chance to learn craftsmanship, customer service and business skills.

Our story

We are a local Prosper family building a business around hard work, clean installs and service homeowners can feel good about supporting.

Projects are completed by motivated student athletes under experienced adult supervision. The goal is simple: deliver a premium result for homeowners while helping young men learn responsibility, communication and pride in their work.
