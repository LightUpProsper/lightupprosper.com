About Us

Local students. Premium lighting. Honest work.

Light Up Prosper was created to provide homeowners with beautiful, professionally installed landscape lighting while giving local students the opportunity to learn business, craftsmanship, and customer service.

Our team is made up of hardworking Prosper-area student athletes who take pride in every installation. Every project is carefully planned and completed under experienced adult supervision.
