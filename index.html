Why Light Up Prosper

Built by Students. Backed by Family.

Light Up Prosper was founded by local students with a simple goal: build something they could be proud of while serving their community.

From meeting homeowners and creating lighting plans to installing fixtures and making final nighttime adjustments, the students are involved throughout every project.

Behind them are supportive families, experienced mentors, and trusted supplier partners who help guide the process—but the vision, effort, and pride belong to the students.

Every Great Project Starts With a Plan

Every lighting plan is carefully designed before installation.

1

Consultation

We walk your property, learn how you use your outdoor spaces, and discuss what you'd like to highlight.

2

Custom Design

We determine fixture placement, beam angles, and lighting effects before the first fixture goes into the ground.

3

Installation & Reveal

Our student team installs the system, then aims and fine-tunes every fixture after dark until the lighting feels balanced.
