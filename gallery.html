Featured Projects

Every home has a story. These projects show how thoughtful lighting can bring warmth, depth, safety, and curb appeal to outdoor spaces after sunset.

Featured Project 001

Where It All Started

This backyard project sparked the idea for Light Up Prosper. One of our families wanted to transform an already beautiful pool into a space that could be enjoyed long after the sun went down.

That first project showed us that landscape lighting isn't about adding more light. It's about highlighting the things that make a property special.

Featured Project 002

Our Home. Our Test Lab.

Before asking customers to trust us with their homes, we wanted to prove our process on our own. This project gave us the opportunity to experiment with placement, beam angles, and nighttime adjustments until every light felt intentional.

Featured Project 003

Modern Home. Timeless Warmth.

A beautifully remodeled home deserves to be enjoyed after sunset. This project focused on highlighting clean architecture, mature landscaping, and a welcoming entry while maintaining a warm, understated appearance.
